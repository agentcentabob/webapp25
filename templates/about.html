Our Vision

Urban Notes aims to create a space where urban planning enthusiasts, transit advocates, and city lovers can document their observations, share insights, and build a community around our shared passion for better cities.

We believe that every perspective matters in shaping the cities of tomorrow. Whether you're documenting transport systems, analyzing urban design, or sharing your favorite city moments, Urban Notes is your digital companion.
